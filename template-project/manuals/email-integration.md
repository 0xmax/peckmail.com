# Email Integration

Every workspace gets a unique email address. When someone sends an email to your workspace, an AI agent reads it and takes action based on your instructions in `AGENTS.md`.

## Finding your workspace email

Go to **Settings** (gear icon in the sidebar) to see your workspace's email address. It looks something like `robin-willow-42@in.perchpad.co`.

## How it works

1. Someone sends an email to your workspace address
2. The AI agent reads the email content
3. It follows the instructions in your `AGENTS.md` file
4. Files are created or updated in your workspace automatically
5. The agent can reply to the sender to confirm what it did

## Customizing the agent

Edit the `AGENTS.md` file in your project root to change how the agent handles incoming emails. You can set up rules for different types of content, specify where files should be saved, and define how the agent should respond.

## Ideas

- Forward newsletters to your workspace for automatic summarization
- Email yourself recipes and have them auto-filed
- Send meeting notes that get organized into the right folders
- Forward interesting articles to build a reading list
