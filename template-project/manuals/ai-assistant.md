# AI Assistant

Your workspace comes with a built-in AI assistant powered by Claude. Open the chat panel on the right side of the editor to start a conversation.

## What it can do

- **Read your files** — Ask it to summarize, review, or analyze anything in your workspace
- **Edit your files** — It can make changes, fix typos, restructure content, or rewrite sections
- **Create new files** — Ask it to draft documents, generate templates, or scaffold content
- **Search your workspace** — It can grep across files, find patterns, and locate specific content
- **Send emails** — It can email your workspace collaborators
- **Invite people** — Ask it to invite someone by email to join your workspace

## Tips

- The assistant is aware of which file you have open and where your cursor is, so you can say things like "fix the typo on this line" or "expand this section"
- It's great for brainstorming — just describe what you're working on and ask for ideas
- For CSV files, you can ask it to add rows, sort data, or create new columns

## Thinking mode

Toggle the brain icon in the chat to enable thinking mode. The assistant will show its reasoning process before responding — useful for complex tasks.
