# Welcome to Perchpad!

Hey there! Welcome to your new workspace. This is your little corner of the internet for writing, organizing, and thinking out loud.

Here's what you'll find in this starter workspace:

- **manuals/** — Quick guides on everything Perchpad can do
- **recipes/** — A few of our favorite dishes to get your collection started
- **reading/** — Reading list and movie watchlist templates
- **todo/** — Task lists to keep you organized
- **sandbox/** — A playground with some fun creative writing to poke around in
- **inbox/** — Where emails sent to your workspace land
- **AGENTS.md** — Instructions for the AI email agent (feel free to customize!)

## Getting started

Just click any file in the sidebar to start editing. Everything saves automatically and is version-controlled — so you can never lose your work.

Open the chat panel on the right to talk to your AI assistant. It can read and edit your files, help you brainstorm, proofread your writing, or even send emails to your teammates.

Happy writing!
