# Movie Watchlist

## Want to watch

- **Past Lives** (2023) — Two childhood friends from Seoul reconnect in New York decades later
- **The Holdovers** (2023) — A cranky teacher stuck at boarding school over Christmas with a troubled student
- **Perfect Days** (2023) — A Tokyo toilet cleaner who finds beauty in routine and small pleasures
- **All of Us Strangers** (2023) — A screenwriter visits his childhood home and finds his parents alive
- **Aftersun** (2022) — A woman rewatches home videos from a holiday with her father

## Favorites

- **In the Mood for Love** (2000) — Two neighbors discover their spouses are having an affair. Restraint, longing, and gorgeous cinematography.
- **Spirited Away** (2001) — A girl trapped in a spirit world bathhouse. Miyazaki at his best.
- **Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind** (2004) — What if you could erase someone from your memory?
- **Arrival** (2016) — A linguist learns an alien language that changes how she experiences time.
- **Moonlight** (2016) — Three chapters in the life of a young Black man growing up in Miami.
- **The Grand Budapest Hotel** (2014) — A legendary concierge and his lobby boy get wrapped up in art theft and murder.
