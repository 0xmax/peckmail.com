# Reading List

## Currently reading

- **Project Hail Mary** by Andy Weir — Astronaut wakes up alone on a spaceship with no memory. Solving impossible problems with science and an unlikely friend.

## Want to read

- **Piranesi** by Susanna Clarke — A man lives in a house with infinite halls and an ocean in the basement
- **Tomorrow, and Tomorrow, and Tomorrow** by Gabrielle Zevin — Two friends build video games together across decades
- **The Overstory** by Richard Powers — Nine strangers whose lives are changed by trees
- **Klara and the Sun** by Kazuo Ishiguro — An artificial friend observes the human world from a store window
- **The Memory Police** by Yoko Ogawa — On an island where things keep disappearing from existence

## Finished

- **Dune** by Frank Herbert — Desert planet, giant worms, political intrigue. The sci-fi epic.
- **The Left Hand of Darkness** by Ursula K. Le Guin — A human envoy on a planet where people have no fixed gender
- **Educated** by Tara Westover — Growing up survivalist in Idaho, then finding your way to Cambridge
- **The Remains of the Day** by Kazuo Ishiguro — A butler reflects on a life of service and missed chances
