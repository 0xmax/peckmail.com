# Ira's Hydroponics Cookbook — Recipes for Deep Space

*Compiled by Ira Moss, Ship's Botanist, ISV Meadowlark*

## Zero-G Tomato Soup

You haven't lived until you've chased a globule of hot soup through the galley during an artificial gravity malfunction.

**Ingredients**: Deck 5 cherry tomatoes (the good ones, not the weird pale ones from Batch 7), dried basil from the herb wall, one packet of reconstituted cream, garlic powder, love.

**Method**: Blend everything. Heat until it stops tasting like sadness. Serve in sealed containers because Koa still hasn't fixed the gravity compensator in the mess hall.

## The Fen Aldric Memorial Salad

Named after the time Fen accidentally ate an entire experimental lettuce cultivar that was supposed to last the whole crew a week.

**Ingredients**: Whatever's growing. Seriously, just grab a handful of greens. Add some of those micro-radishes. Squeeze a lemon over it. Done.

**Fen's note**: "In my defense, it was delicious and I regret nothing."

## Kit's Morale Cookies

Kit claims these cure homesickness. Results are inconclusive but the placebo effect is strong.

**Ingredients**: Shelf-stable flour, reconstituted butter, sugar ration (save up for two weeks), chocolate chips (barter with Tomas — he always has a secret stash in Cargo Bay 2).

**Method**: Standard cookie procedure. The smell alone is worth the sugar ration. Bake at whatever temperature the oven is currently running at (Koa says it's 180C but we all know it runs hot).
