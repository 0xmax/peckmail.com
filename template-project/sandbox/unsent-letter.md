# Unsent Letter

*Found tucked into the maintenance manual for Deck 4's water recycler. Author unknown.*

---

I keep meaning to send this but the relay queue is 11 days long and by the time you read it everything will be different anyway.

We found something out here. I can't say what yet — the captain wants to wait for Fleet Command's response before we go public. But I want you to know that all those nights in the observatory, all those hours we spent arguing about the Fermi paradox over bad coffee — you were right. We're not alone. We were never alone.

The thing is, knowing doesn't feel the way I thought it would. I expected trumpets and champagne. Instead it feels quiet. Almost sad. Like finding a note from someone who left the party before you arrived.

Ira's tomatoes are coming in beautifully. We had bruschetta last Tuesday. Koa fixed the shower on Deck 3 so we don't have to use the one next to engineering anymore (you remember the one that sounded like a dying whale). Kit taught Biscuit to sit on command, though Biscuit only does it when she feels like it, so arguably Kit taught Biscuit nothing.

The stars are different out here. Fewer of them. It makes the ones you can see feel more important somehow.

I miss you. I'll send this tomorrow. Or the day after.
