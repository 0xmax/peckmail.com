# Thai Green Curry

Fragrant, spicy, and ready in 30 minutes. This one's a weeknight lifesaver.

## Ingredients

- 400ml coconut milk
- 3 tablespoons green curry paste
- 500g chicken thigh, sliced (or tofu for vegetarian)
- 1 eggplant, cubed
- 100g green beans, trimmed
- Handful of Thai basil leaves
- 2 kaffir lime leaves, torn
- 1 tablespoon fish sauce
- 1 tablespoon palm sugar (or brown sugar)
- 1 red chili, sliced
- Jasmine rice to serve

## Steps

1. Scoop the thick cream from the top of the coconut milk into a hot wok. Stir until it splits and gets oily.
2. Add the curry paste and fry in the coconut cream for 2 minutes until very fragrant.
3. Add the chicken and cook until just sealed on all sides.
4. Pour in the rest of the coconut milk. Add the eggplant and lime leaves.
5. Simmer for 10 minutes until the eggplant is tender.
6. Add the green beans. Cook 3 more minutes.
7. Season with fish sauce and sugar. Taste and adjust.
8. Stir in the Thai basil. Top with sliced chili.
9. Serve over jasmine rice.

## Notes

If you can find fresh green peppercorns, throw a handful in with the eggplant. They add an incredible pop of heat.
