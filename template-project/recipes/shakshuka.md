# Shakshuka

Eggs poached in a spiced tomato sauce. Perfect for breakfast, lunch, or a lazy dinner.

## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1 onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes
- Handful of fresh cilantro
- Crumbled feta cheese
- Crusty bread for dipping
- Olive oil, salt, pepper

## Steps

1. Heat olive oil in a deep skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and bell pepper. Cook until soft, about 5 minutes.
2. Add the garlic, cumin, paprika, and chili flakes. Stir for a minute.
3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes until slightly thickened.
4. Make six wells in the sauce with the back of a spoon. Crack an egg into each well.
5. Cover and cook on low for 5-8 minutes until the whites are set but the yolks are still runny.
6. Scatter feta and cilantro over the top.
7. Bring the whole skillet to the table with warm bread.

## Notes

This serves 2-3 people generously. Scale up the sauce if feeding a crowd — just use a bigger pan.
